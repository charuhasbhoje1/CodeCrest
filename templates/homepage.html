About Globein
The Global Company News Aggregator App acts as a personal market intelligence assistant for MSMEs. It collects, organizes, and summarizes company-specific and industry-wide news from across the world into one simple app.
Target Audience
This app targets small and medium enterprises that need market awareness but lack research resources
Marketing Strategy:
• Collaborate with MSME associations, export promotion councils, and startup incubators.
• Freemium model to attract mass adoption, with premium plans for deeper analytics.
• Use case studies showing how real MSMEs improved decision-making and sales with the app
